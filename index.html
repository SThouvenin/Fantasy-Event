Event Information


Stacy McQuerrey is a photographer from Galion, Ohio. She has the biggest heart of anyone I have ever met. This shoot is being put together as a benefit to help her and her family, and every penny will go to help her and her family. Stacy is an amazing photographer, and friend to many.

For those of you that don't know Stacy well, but have seen her work - She is planning to sell her photography equipment to try to keep a roof over her family's head. She and her husband both work full-time and parent 3 kids. She fell sick at the end of last year with a blood clot in her leg and pneumonia, that's when the medical bills started to catch up with them. PLEASE help them keep a roof over their heads. She has had many people rallying around her, so please spread the word so we help her in anyway we can. She was just given the total that needs paid and it is a large sum, She needs $4,000 by June 23rd in order to keep her home.

**MEETING PLACE** - Stacy's Brother's at 29771 Rabbit Ridge Rd Howard, Ohio 43028! Exact Address will be listed here soon!

The theme of the shoot is Fantasy- but photographers and models are free to create their own ideas and styles. It has been amazing to see everyone rallying together to support one of their own in the photography community.

The cost of attending is a minimum $25 donation! (Hair and makeup get to come free!) I will be setting up PayPal for it in the next days and will be keeping track! Please do not schedule with photographers or models if you have not yet made a contribution!

MAKE-UP ARTISTS
Please consider attending the event - We are not requesting any donations from make-up artists, so please consider attending, and please post on the page regarding your rates! This is an excellent networking opportunity as well.

PHOTOGRAPHERS
Please understand the name of the shoot is Fantasy- but you can go with your own style and visions. Being in a private location - Nudes and artistic nudes are permitted. This is a trade event so please communicate with the models what they can expect to receive in regards to photos. ie- 5 edited shots, watermark or no watermark, how many outfit changes there will be. If you have them, I recommend bringing a release. These negotiations will be between you and the model, and I will not set expectations and deadlines. PLEASE stay in communication with the models after the shoot. We also will love behind the scenes photos!

MODELS
After the event, models will receive the best edited images. The negotiations over picture will be between you and the photographer. Feel free to roam around and hang out, there will be food, friends and fun! Though the theme is Fantasy - Bring any style of clothing and go ham!

WARDROBE
Anyone attending as a wardrobe stylist and not a photographer or model, can attend at no charge. The wardrobist is welcome to sell their product at the event as well. A wardrobe person can also rent their wares.

PETS
Please don't bring your pets along - but by request you may be able to shoot with a beautiful Husky Named Sasha if Jay, Stacy's brother is willing!

INTERIOR SHOOTING
The interior areas for shooting are the barn and the open building. The barn will be the only indoor spot with electricity.

NUDE SHOOTING
Nude & implied shooting is ALLOWED in the barn and in the out areas out of view of our staging/meeting area and the main road. Keep in mind that minors are permitted at this event and if you are a photographer that will be shooting nude models, you must register with the staff at check in. Also acceptable is the treeline and in the woods, behind things out of view from the road/neighbors, fully acceptable (in other words, out of sight from the main area). So if you can see the road and the neighbors, then they can see you. In the barn nudes completely acceptable. Now the state park up the road with the waterfall, I would suggest no nudes, since you are no longer on the event property and at the scrutiny of the public and the rangers. We ask photographers, If requesting nude shooting with a model, always show respect, please request in private messaging, and ASK A MODEL ONLY ONCE, if the model says no, then NO MEANS NO. Any reported pressuring, persuasive overtly sexual language, or harassment of any kind will result in dismissal from the event.

RAIN DATE
We will set a rain date if this one does not work.
